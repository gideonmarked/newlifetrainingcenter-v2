Admission to NTC is open to:

  • Members of Newlife Christian Center Alabang
  • Newlife satellite churches and affiliates,
  • Born-again Christian believers from other churches, both national and international.

Admission Requirements:

Day School (Full-time and Pastoral Track)
  • Online application form
  • Scanned Pastoral Recommendation
  • Scanned Commitment to Honor Form
  • Scanned NBI Clearance (issued within 3 months prior to enrollment)
  • Scanned Medical clearance (issued within 3 months prior to enrollment)
  • Interview
  • Training Fee: Php 2,000.00 monthly
International Students
  • Would be required to show proof of legal permission to reside and/or study in the Philippines
  • Online application form
  • Scanned Pastoral Recommendation
  • Scanned Commitment to Honor Form
  • Scanned NBI Clearance (issued within 3 months prior to enrollment)
  • Scanned Medical clearance (issued within 3 months prior to enrollment)
  • Interview
  • Training Fee: Php 2,000.00 monthly
Note All international students bear the sole responsibility for compliance to Philippine immigration requirements. NTC is not responsible for monthly expenses, including food and lodging.
Blockweek Nights @ NTC
  • Training Fee: PHP 400.00 per subject
Note For NCC Alabang staff: NBI and Medical clearances are not required. NTC and its programs are not recognized by any Philippine educational regulatory body.
