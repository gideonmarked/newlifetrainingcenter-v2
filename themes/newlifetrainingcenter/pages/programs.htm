The annual school year, for both day school and blockweek nights, runs from June through March, divided into a quarterly system.

Day School

Full time study
The training period is a 10-month commitment
Tuesdays to Fridays, 8:30 am – 12:30 pm
Open to both Newlife and non-Newlife members

Pastoral Track

A study program for Senior Pastors of Newlife satellite churches.

BlockWeek Nights @ NTC

Open to both Newlife and non-Newlife members
Scheduled during one pre-selected week in a month
Tuesday to Friday evenings, 7:00 pm – 9:00 pm
